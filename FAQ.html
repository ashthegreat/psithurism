Got Questions?

What payment method can I use?

We accept all major credit and debit cards as well as Paypal.

Can I use my subscription on more than one device?

Your subscription gives you access on up to 5 devices simultaneously.

Can I get a digital subscription to the Irish News website only?

Each digital subscription package gives readers ongoing access to www.irishnews.com on any computer, tablet or mobile device and includes access to the Irish News Apps available on IOS and Android.

Can I subscribe for specific days e.g. can I read it every Thursday for a month?

Sorry, no. Each digital subscription gives readers full access to www.irishnews.com 24 hours per day, 7 days per week.

Can I share my subscription with anyone else?

Your subscription gives you access on up to 5 devices simultaneously so you can share with your family.

I already have an online subscription. Do I have to pay again to access the website?

Your existing subscription to our digital editions / apps will also give you access to www.irishnews.com. You can use the same login details.

Can I subscribe to the family notices only?

Sorry, no. Each digital subscription gives readers full access to www.irishnews.com which includes the family notices.

If I go back and read an article I've already read, will that count toward my monthly limit?

If you view an article you have already read in the current month, it will not count again toward your monthly limit.

What if I don't want to subscribe — can I still read irishnews.com for free?

Registered readers can enjoy 10 free articles (including blog posts, slide shows and other multimedia features) each month on www.irishnews.com. Your free, limited access resets every 30 days.
